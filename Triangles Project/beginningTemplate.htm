Welcome to the Program! In this lesson, you will learn some fundamental concepts behind the study of geometry. Many geometric theorems or principles are derived from the study of triangles, which we will focus on in this brief lecture. In addition, many properties of quadrilaterals, pentagons, hexagons and other types of polygons you will encounter later on in your geometry course can be proved by using triangles...
