In order to establish that two triangles are congruent, we must be confident that they have the same corresponding sidelengths and angle measures

Click to Continue

One way to do this is to see if they if two triangles can be placed on top of one another such that there are no overlapping areas. If this is the case, then all the corresponding segments and angles of one triangle meet at the same endpoints of the other triangle. The act of placing one triangle on top of another and allowing all of this to happen is called SUPERIMPOSITION
